RFM R1T framer tooling change

Scope of work

  • Replace original tooling models
  • Tooling kinematics
  • Tooling valves and process sequence
  • Update robot paths and OLP’s to accommodate new tooling in simulation

Purpose

The current End-of-Arm Tooling (EOAT) for Robots 40 RB 7 & 8 cannot accurately control the position of weld nuts on underbody panels, leading to consistent quality defects. Maintenance identified this issue and requested engineering support to design and implement a replacement tooling solution that ensures proper nut location.
